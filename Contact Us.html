FAQ'S

What makes ‘Save the Turtles’ unique?

Save the Turtles is an all-volunteer non-profit organisation that has been saving endangered sea turtles since 2015. We are committed to raising money and spreading awareness of the conditions that turtles are currently in. One of the things that is most important to us is keeping our supporters updated on any and all developments made through our organisation on our social platforms. We are the longest running Irish organisation of our kind and, we believe, one of the most experienced. We are all passionate about saving this species before it’s too late. This combination of experience and passion means that we are hardworking and excited to get you involved.

How long have you been going?

Save the Turtles was born in 2015, founded by Tadhg Ryan, Amelia Lawlor, Kewin Barczak and Caoimhe Cahill. Visit our 'About Us' page to learn more about our founders.

How do I know ‘Save the Turtles’ is a responsible organisation?

Save the Turtles is proud to be leading the way in protecting turtles in many ways. Through visiting our social platforms, you can witness the work and projects we do every week and events that you can join in on such as beach cleaning. By visiting our 'Get Involved' page or looking at the question below, you can see what exactly your donations are used for.

Where does my money go?

We run a tight ship at our main office when it comes to your money. Here is a general break down of where your money will go. Nearly all funds raised goes to direct protection of sea turtles. Funds are used for travel when going to many beaches in Ireland and Europe when helping baby turtles to get to sea safely from predators. Donations are also used to help clean the ocean of waste products and supplying food for large groups of turtles in specific areas due to the water pollution.

I don’t live in Ireland, can I still donate?

YES!! We take donations from people from all around the world. In the past 12 months, we’ve received funds from many Australians, Americans, Canadians and Europeans.

What is the minimum amount I can donate?

The minimum amount you can donate is €5 by texting ‘SAVE’ to (+353) 88 321 5431. The minimum amount you can donate online through card is €10. Check out our 'Get Involved' page for more information about donations.
