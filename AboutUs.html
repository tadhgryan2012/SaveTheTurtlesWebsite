About Us

Meet the Team!

Founded in 2015. A group of ocean loving teenagers took on a challenge of a lifetime. What started as a local project has expanded to national level in Ireland with major plans for expansion worldwide. With over 70 volunteers, much work is to be done. The organisation organises events and fundraisers on the daily to aid the clean up of the ocean.
