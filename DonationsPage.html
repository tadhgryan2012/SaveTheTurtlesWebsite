Getting involved !

There are many ways in which YOU can get involved ! Every bit of help contributes to the project.

The main form of help would be by donating and you can do this directly by paypal or using the mobile service below.

For every €1, 10 turtle hatchlings can be saved. We aim to protect the newborn turtles before they reach the sea because only 1 out of 1,000 hatchlings reach adulthood !

Our details for the paypal can be found here.

If you do not have access to a credit/debit card , don't worry!

You can also text 'SAVE' to (+353) 88 321 5431.
This will charge you just €5 with no additional costs!
